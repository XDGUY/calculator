How to use

  1. Choose the distribution to calculate: 1 = Binomial, 2 = Geometric, 3 = Poisson.
  2. Enter the probability or λ(for Poisson distribution)
  3. Enter the range (C≤X≤D)
  4. enter the number of trails kif using Binomial distribution
  5. answer is saved in M
